Stories of love


Made with love... and taste


Il coronavirus ci ha tolto molte cose, ma agli italiani niente e nessuno potrà mai togliere il buon cibo e il gusto di prepararlo!
Questo è un piatto tipico della tradizione emiliana, e in particolare piacentina. Noi lo chiamiamo "anolino", ed è il cibo delle festività. Durante la quarantena, un grande sfogo è stata la possibilità di preparare dei piatti fatti a mano. Io e la mia famiglia ci siamo divertiti a fare questo!

The coronavirus has taken away many things, but nothing and no one can ever take away good food and the taste of preparing it from Italians!
This is a typical dish of the Emilian tradition, and in particular of Piacenza. We call it "anolino", and it's the festivities food. During the quarantine, a great outlet was the opportunity to prepare handmade dishes. My family and I had fun doing this!

Martina Boiardi, 27th March 2020, Chero (PC)

About IMOW

IN MY OWN WORDS is an original project about a virtual museum dedicated to the global-wide event of the COVID-19 emergency situtation. IMOW is aimed at collecting in a single virtual place the memory of this time: celebrating its collective aspect as well as preserving its single features.
Because it's the first time no one is a simple visitor.
